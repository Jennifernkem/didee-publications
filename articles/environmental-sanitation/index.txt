10:T457,This study determined the extent to which environmental sanitation activities are utilized in resolving environmental issues for sustainable community development in South East States, Nigeria. Results revealed that sanitation activities were utilized to a high extent in resolving environmental issues for sustainable community development (grand mean = 3.27). No significant difference was found between male and female members' ratings (t-cal = 0.23, t-crit = ±1.96).
